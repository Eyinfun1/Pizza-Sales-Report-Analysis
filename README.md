# Pizza Sales Report Analysis (2015)

##  Overview
This project analyzes pizza sales performance for the year **2015 (January – December)**.  
It focuses on identifying sales trends, revenue performance, customer preferences, and business insights.

---

##  Objectives
- Identify the day with the highest number of orders  
- Determine pizzas with the highest and lowest revenue  
- Analyze lunch hour orders (11 AM – 3 PM)  
- Identify highest and lowest revenue months  
- Evaluate quarterly performance  
- Discover the most preferred pizza  

---

##  Dataset
The dataset was sourced from **Kaggle** and contains four tables:
- Pizzas  
- Pizza Types  
- Orders  
- Order Details  

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning, Analysis, Visualization)

---

##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Orders:** 48,620  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 49,574  
- **Total Sales:** 801,945  
- **December Revenue:** 64,701 (-8% vs last month)  

---

##  Top 5 Pizzas by Revenue
- Thai Chicken Pizza — ₦43,434  
- Barbecue Chicken Pizza — ₦42,768  
- California Pizza — ₦41,409  
- Spicy Italian Pizza — ₦34,831  
- Classic Deluxe Pizza — ₦31,180  

---

##  Bottom Performing Pizzas
- Brie Carre Pizza — ₦11,588  
- Spinach Supreme Pizza — ₦15,277  
- Mediterranean Pizza — ₦15,360.50  
- Spinach Pesto Pizza — ₦15,596  

---

## Customer Preferences
- Most preferred category: **Classic**  
- Followed by: Supreme and Chicken  
- Least preferred: Veggie  

---

##  Pizza Sizes
- Most ordered: Large  
- Followed by: Medium  
- Least ordered: Extra Large & XXL  

---

##  Order Insights
- **Busiest day:** Friday  
- Followed by: Thursday and Saturday  

---

##  Peak Hours
- 12 PM — Highest orders  
- 1 PM — Second highest  

---

##  Monthly Trend
- Highest sales: January  
- Declines: February, April, June  
- Drop: August – October  
- Recovery: November  
- Decline: December  

---

##  Lunch Hour Analysis (11 AM – 3 PM)
- 3 PM — 54%  
- 11 AM — 46%  

---

##  Quarterly Performance
- Q1 — Growth phase  
- Q2 — Peak performance  
- Q3 & Q4 — Decline  

---

##  Conclusion
The business performed strongly in Q2 and during lunch hours.  
Thai Chicken Pizza generated the highest revenue, while Classic pizzas were the most preferred.

Revenue declined toward the end of the year, indicating opportunities for promotions and improved marketing strategies.
